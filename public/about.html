Our Story

Can you throw a skip card in tandem with a draw four in Uno? What’s the max number of jumps you can make in one turn checkers? When playing hide and seek, are you found when you’re seen or when you’re tagged? For centuries, these questions have fractured families and ended friendships. Here at Stickler’s Paradise, we wanted remedy some of the conflict in a world by creating an open source one-stop-shop for game rules. We provide a space for sticklers everywhere to publish their version of game protocols, as well as view the rules that others have published. Make your clearly correct voice heard. And as always, play in peace friends!
